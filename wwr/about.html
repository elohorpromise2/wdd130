Exciting Expeditions

At Exciting Expeditions, our mission is to design guided river experiences that blend safety, skill‑building, and pure fun. Whether it’s your first splash or your next big rapid, our professional guides help you read water, build confidence, and create memories that last.

History

Founded in 1996 by a small crew of river enthusiasts, Exciting Expeditions grew from weekend runs into a full‑season operation. What began as a single inflatable and a borrowed trailer has evolved into a well‑equipped fleet and an unwavering commitment to responsible, small‑group river travel.
