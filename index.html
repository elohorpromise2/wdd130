Exciting Expeditions

Our Mission

At Exciting Expeditions, our mission is to design guided river experiences that blend safety, skill-building, and pure fun. Whether it’s a first splash or a next big rapid, our professional guides help guests read water, build confidence, and create memories that last well beyond the take-out.

History

Founded in 1996 by a small crew of river enthusiasts, Exciting Expeditions grew from weekend runs into a full-season operation. What began as a single inflatable and a borrowed trailer evolved into a well-equipped fleet and an unwavering commitment to responsible, small-group river travel.
